Description

The music player instrument is a visually appealing and interactive experience, resembling a record player with a rotating disc and album covers.

Users can customize volume, frequency, and timbre, blending traditional and modern music elements. Design enhancements include adjustable record rotation speed for synchronized visual representation, AI-generated unique album covers, and a vintage scratch effect for an authentic sound.

Operating instructions involve necessary libraries, sliders for volume, frequency, and timbre, and song selection with the spacebar for pause/resume. Royalty-free songs from Bensound.com are used.
